About The App

Smart Community Support is a modern solution that transforms how communities report and resolve local issues.

Our platform leverages cutting-edge technology to streamline communication between residents and local authorities. With real-time issue tracking and AI-powered insights, we're building smarter, more responsive communities.

Citizens can report problems like road damage, water issues, garbage overflow, and streetlight outages in just a few taps. Each report is automatically categorized and routed to the appropriate department for quick resolution.

Authorities benefit from a comprehensive dashboard that provides actionable insights, priority-based task management, and performance analytics to improve public services.
